2022年1月英语单词

1月2日

EnglishPod

  1. be still working on
  • Did you finish reading the magazine?
  • I’m still working on it.
  1. coke and fries = coke cola and French fires
  2. complimentary glass of wine = free
  3. I’ll go with sth = I’ll choose/take/pick sth
  4. grab sth = go get sth quickly
  • Wait me please, I need to grab my coat.
  • Let’s grab a cup of coffee.
  1. How soon is soon?

Daily English

Mother Teresa, who received a Nobel Peace Prize for her work on behalf of the poor, dies in Calcutta, India - she was 87 years old.

1月3日

EnglishPod—calling in sick

  1. sore throat, runny nose
  2. coming down with = starting to get sick
  • My throat really hurts, I think I’m coming down with a cold.
  • I don’t feel well, I think I’m coming down with something.
  1. calling in sick
  2. take the day-off = not go to work

Daily English

  • Jerry, what time do you have? do you = dya
  • I have 5 o’clock.

1月4日

EnglishPod—Hotel Upgrade

  1. under the name (go to hotel…)
  • My secretary made a reservation under my name.
  • I’m sorry, sir, I don’t have a reservation under that name.
  1. mix-up
  • I’m sorry sir, there’s been a terrible mix-up. We’re giving you the wrong baby.

  1. be overbooked = full, more than full (hotel, flight, bus, train…)

  2. complimentary upgrade = free upgrade

Daily English

  • There are three things I have learned never to discuss with people: religion, politics and the Great Pumpkin!

1月9日

EnglishPod—The office—I need an assistant!

  1. the timing is just not right
  • W: Honey, I think we should have a baby.
  • M: The timing is just not right.
  1. weight off my shoulders

Daily English

  • Don’t ever let somebody tell you, you can’t do something. sump’n

Strong sounds: S, N, L;

Weak sounds: d, t, th; usually canceled.

1月13日

Daily English

  • I have come here to chew bubblegum and kick ass… and I’m all out of bubblegum!

  1. to: to, tah, tuh, t’, dah, duh, d’ here to = here d’

  2. and I’m = anIm

  3. out of = outta
  • The secret of life is just to live every moment. secret [ˈsiːkrət]
  1. just = juss or jus
  2. just to = juss t’

1月14日

Daily English

  • Another rogue trader has cost a bank billions.
  1. rogue [rəʊɡ] 无赖; 捣蛋鬼; 骗子; 恶棍; 流氓
  2. Smoke costs your life.

EnglishPod—cut in line

  1. so sue me = 在口语中它是一句“不服气”的话，指某人根本“不在乎自己说的这句话/这件事是否令对方气恼”。（一般是觉得自己做的事情没有错），完整句式是”(so) sue me.”

  2. cut in line = jump in queue
