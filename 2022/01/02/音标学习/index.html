英音音标学习笔记

单元音

/ɪ/

  • hit, sick, lip, will, bitch

/i:/

  • fleece, sea, machine, beach
  • similar sounds:
    • /ɪ/: litter, chip, fit, his, pitch, ship
    • /i:/: liter, cheap, feet, he’s, peach, sheap

/ʊ/

  • foot, put, good

/u:/

  • goose, two, blue, group

/ʌ/

  • strut, mud, love, blood

/ɑ:/

  • father, start, hard

/ɒ/

  • lot, odd, wash

/ɔ:/

  • thought, law, north, war

/ə/

  • This is the most important sound in English.

  • a, the, of, that 后两个是弱读形式

  • about, common, standard, butter

schwa: [ʃwɑː] n.非重读央元音，弱央元音，混元音(如about中的a或moment中的e所发的音); 音标符号 /ə/ ;

  • acuse, banana, father, camera

  • cuppa = cup of

/ɜ:/

  • nurse, stur, learn, refer

/e/

  • dress, head, bed, many
  • similar sounds:
    • /ɪ/: bid, middel, did, fill, pin
    • /e/: bed, medal, dead, fell, pen

/æ/

  • trap, stamp, back, lap

双元音

/ɪə/

  • ear, near, hear

/ʊə/

  • tour, pure, cure

/aɪ/

  • eye, price, high, try

/ɔɪ/

  • choice, noise, voice, boy

/əʊ/

  • boat, show, know

/eə/

  • square, fair, pair

/aʊ/

  • mouth, now

/eɪ/

  • face, day, break, great

辅音

/p/

  • pack, pan, copy, happen, hop, pop

/b/

  • bob, back, bag, hobby, habit, job

/t/

  • tin, tight, to, eight

/d/
